# Absorbed Audio/Music Skills

This reference records music/audio skills consolidated into `audio-music-workflows`.

- `songwriting-and-ai-music` — song structure, rhyme/meter, emotional arc, lyrics, parody/adaptation, and Suno prompt engineering.
- `audiocraft` / candidate `audiocraft-audio-generation` — AudioCraft MusicGen/AudioGen setup, text-to-music, and text-to-sound generation.
- `heartmula` — HeartMuLa setup, hardware requirements, and Suno-like generation workflow.
- `songsee` — audio spectrograms and features such as mel, chroma, and MFCC.

The umbrella separates creative prompting, generation, and analysis.
