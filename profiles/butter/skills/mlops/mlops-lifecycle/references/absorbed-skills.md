- `huggingface-hub` — Hugging Face Hub model/dataset workflows.
- `llama-cpp` — local GGUF inference, server, optimization, quantization references.
- `weights-and-biases` — W&B experiment tracking, sweeps, artifacts, integrations.

- `segment-anything-model` — SAM zero-shot segmentation workflows.
- `serving-llms-vllm` — vLLM OpenAI-compatible serving and throughput workflows.

Some package directory basenames differed from their frontmatter names (`audiocraft`, `lm-evaluation-harness`, `segment-anything`, `vllm`); they were archived by their package paths after frontmatter verification.
