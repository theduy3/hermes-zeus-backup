# Travel Workouts — Hotel Room Bodyweight

Zero equipment. 30 minutes. Shoulder-safe.

## The Session (30 min)

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|---|----------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Bodyweight Squat | 3 × 15-20 | Slow tempo, 3 sec down |
| 2 | Push-Ups (knees if needed) | 3 × AMRAP | Stop 1 rep before failure. Against desk/wall if floor triggers shoulder |
| 3 | Reverse Lunge | 3 × 10/leg | Hold water bottle |
| 4 | Glute Bridge | 3 × 12-15 | Single-leg if possible, 2 sec hold at top |
| 5 | Doorway Row | 3 × 10-12 | Grip doorframe, lean back, pull chest to frame |
| 6 | Plank to Downward Dog | 3 × 5 transitions | Core + shoulder mobility |
| 7 | Isometric Wall Press | 3 × 20 sec/side | Back of hand into wall at shoulder height |

## Nutrition: Travel MVP
- Protein-first ordering at every restaurant meal
- Whey shake brunch (hotel coffee + whey = 30g protein)
- 2-drink alcohol max
- Never miss twice rule active

## Packing List
- Whey protein (pre-portioned ziplocks)
- Resistance band (light)
- Lacrosse ball (trap/shoulder release)

Home recovery sessions (Tue/Thu/Sat) require no equipment and travel perfectly.
