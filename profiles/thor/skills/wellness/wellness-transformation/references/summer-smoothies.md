# Summer Smoothie Brunch Options (IF-Adapted)

Each targets 40-50g protein. Designed for intermittent fasting (brunch = first meal).

## 5 Smoothie Brunch Recipes

| # | Name | Ingredients | Protein |
|---|------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Tropical Green | 250ml coconut water, 1 scoop vanilla whey, 1 cup frozen mango, handful spinach, 1/2 banana, 1 tbsp chia seeds | ~35g (+15g Greek yogurt) |
| 2 | Berry Almond | 250ml unsweetened almond milk, 1 scoop vanilla whey, 1 cup mixed frozen berries, 1 tbsp almond butter, 30g oats | ~40g |
| 3 | Coffee Protein | 200ml cold brew + 100ml milk, 1 scoop chocolate whey, 1/2 frozen banana, ice | ~35g |
| 4 | Watermelon Mint | 200g watermelon, 1 scoop unflavored/vanilla whey, 100g Greek yogurt, fresh mint, lime juice, ice | ~40g |
| 5 | Peach Ginger | 1 ripe peach, 250ml milk, 1 scoop vanilla whey, 1 tsp grated ginger, 30g oats | ~40g |

## Juice Accents (Not Meal Replacements)

150ml shots only — never 500ml juice meals (spikes blood sugar, no protein).

- Celery + cucumber + lemon + ginger → electrolytes, anti-inflammatory
- Beet + carrot + apple + ginger → natural nitrates for blood flow, pre-workout

## Key Rules
- Smoothies keep fiber → satiety and stable blood sugar
- Juicing removes fiber → use only as accent, not meal
- Always include a protein source (whey, Greek yogurt, or both)
- No "juice cleanse" — you're not detoxing, you're spiking blood sugar
