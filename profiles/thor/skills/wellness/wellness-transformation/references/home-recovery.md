# Home Recovery Sessions — 3×/Week

No equipment. Mat or carpet. Spine-safe, shoulder-safe.

---

## Tuesday — Spine & Posture (30 min)

**Focus:** Turtle neck fix, spinal decompression, posture correction.

| # | Exercise | Duration | Notes |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Cat-Cow | 10 slow breaths | Warm-up |
| 2 | Seated neck rolls | 5 each direction | Warm-up |
| 3 | Shoulder shrugs | 8 reps | Warm-up |
| 4 | Chin Tucks | 2 × 10 | #1 turtle neck fix. Retract chin back, hold 3 sec. |
| 5 | Wall Angels (floor) | 2 × 8 | Lying on floor if wall triggers shoulder. Goalpost arms. |
| 6 | Thread the Needle | 30 sec/side × 2 | Thoracic rotation. |
| 7 | Child's Pose + side reach | 45 sec center, 30 sec/side | Spinal decompression. |
| 8 | Supine Twist | 45 sec/side | QL release, low back. |
| 9 | Sphinx Pose | 3 × 30 sec | Gentle back extension. Counterpose to sitting. |
| 10 | Standing Forward Fold | 2 × 45 sec | Knees soft. Full decompression. |
| 11 | Constructive Rest | 3 min | Cooldown. Breathe into low back. |
| 12 | Box breathing | 5 cycles | 4-4-4-4. |

**Scapular prep (for pull-up support):**
- Scapular Wall Slide: 2 × 8 reps (face wall, forearms slide up/down)
- Prone Y-Raise: 2 × 10 reps (lie face down, Y-position arms, lift)

## Thursday — Lymphatic Flow (30 min)

**Focus:** Lymph movement via breath, gravity, gentle movement.

| # | Exercise | Duration | Notes |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Deep diaphragmatic breathing | 10 breaths | Hand on belly. |
| 2 | Gentle marching | 1 min | High knees, arms swinging. |
| 3 | Rebounding | 2 min | Gentle bounce, balls of feet. Primary lymph pump. |
| 4 | Legs-Up-The-Wall | 3 min | Gravity drains legs. Breathe deep. |
| 5 | Cat-Cow (breath emphasis) | 2 min | Spinal fluid pump. |
| 6 | Downward Dog | 3 × 30 sec | Knees bent if needed. Puppy Pose if shoulder triggers. |
| 7 | Standing side bends | 1 min/side | Opens intercostal lymph channels. |
| 8 | Seated spinal twist + deep breath | 1 min/side | Wrings out organs. |
| 9 | Rebounding (round 2) | 2 min | Bookend pump. |
| 10 | Self-massage — neck, armpits, groin | 3 min | Light pressure only. Lymph sits just under skin. |
| 11 | Legs-Up-The-Wall (repeat) | 3 min | Cooldown. |
| 12 | Body scan | 2 min | Notice sensation, temperature. |

## Saturday — Core & Release (35 min)

**Focus:** Plank progression, deep core, shoulder/neck release.

### Warm-up (3 min)
- Chin tucks × 10
- Shoulder circles × 8 each way
- Pelvic tilts × 10

### Core (15-18 min)
| Exercise | Sets | Progression |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Dead Bug | 3 × 8/side | Opposite arm/leg, low back flat |
| Bird Dog | 3 × 6/side | Slow, controlled |
| **Plank — current stage** | 3 × as below | ⬇️ |
| Side Plank (knees if needed) | 2 × 20-30 sec/side | Obliques |

### Plank Progression Stages
1. Forearm Plank → 3 × 45 sec (hips square, no sagging)
2. Shoulder Tap Plank → 3 × 8 taps/side (high plank, no rocking)
3. Plank → Downward Dog → 3 × 6 transitions (smooth control)
4. Weighted Plank → 3 × 30-45 sec (2.5→5 kg plate on mid-back)

Advance when 3 consecutive sessions are clean.

### Release (10 min)
| Exercise | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| Upper Trap Release (ball against wall) | 2 min/side |
| Levator Scapulae Stretch | 45 sec/side |
| Doorway Chest Stretch | 2 × 30 sec (two angles) |
| Child's Pose | 2 min |
| Neck self-massage | 1 min |
| Supine breathing | 2 min |
